Forex Trader - User Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to your all-in-one Forex and Stock Trading platform. To get started, register or log in to access your dashboard where you can view stock prices and place buy/sell orders instantly.

  • Use the search bar to find stocks quickly.
  • Adjust records per page to customize your stock list view.
  • Click Buy to view more details.

Portfolio Overview

Your Portfolio provides key statistics like portfolio value, trades made with wealth calculations.

  • My Wealth:Provides you the total value (Invested + Profit/Loss)
  • P/L of stocks:Track the gain or loss per stock.
  • Avg.Price & Current Price:Analyze the current stock value and current market price.

Placing Trades

Trading is simple and fast. Just search for your desired stock, select it, and click the Buy button.

  • Review the current price and stock details before confirming.
  • Use filters to narrow down your choices by exchange or sector.
  • Track your active orders in the portfolio section.

Tips for Successful Trading

  • Always keep an eye on market trends and sector news.
  • Diversify your portfolio to minimize risk.
  • Use stop-loss orders to protect your investments.
  • Review your trading history regularly to improve strategy.
